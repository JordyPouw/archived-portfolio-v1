About

Hi! I'm Jordy Pouw. I studied at the University of Applied Sciences in Amsterdam and graduated with a Bachelor of Communication and Multimedia Design. As a Frontend Developer I love to build stuff for the web. My work experience includes: developing sites in HTML(5), CSS(3) and JavaScript (jQuery). During my graduation internship I worked at SEOshop in a small team of Frontend Developers where I developed multiple e-commerce shops for clients using the platform.

For my side projects I learned CSS extensions (i.e. LESS, SASS), working with build tools (i.e. grunt), the command line and tried different frontend methodologies like SMACCS, BEM and itCSS. Have a look at my resume to find out more about me.
