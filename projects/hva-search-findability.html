HvA Search and Findability

↫ projects

February 3, 2013

The assignment was to collect 99 items of choice and design a search application for the tablet that had social objects in it. I collected glasses and designed a search application for customers in the store that are searching through the the store's collection to buy some glasses. The customer can reduce the large amount of glasses by filtering properties. This way he finds his new glasses faster and doesn't have to look through the entire store for hours.
